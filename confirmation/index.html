Cleartrip Confirmation page

Cleartrip is an award-winning travel app with over 10 million downloads and hosts about 10 million transactions a year.

The old confirmation page had just become a dumping ground with us trying to help you with anything and everything; trying to sell you everything else we possibly could. This time around, we wanted to avoid that approach and craft a design that would be immune to the future buildup of clutter. We started off by chunking all of the content on the page into three groups &em; your booking details, free and easy add-ons for the trip you just booked and one special offer just for you.
